I am a Research Fellow at the Centre for Political Research at KU Leuven and a Teaching Fellow at the Department of Political Science at the University of Gothenburg. Previously, I held positions as COFAS Marie Curie Fellow in Gothenburg and as Junior Researcher at the University of Twente, where I also obtained by Ph.D. in political science in 2014.

My teaching and research interests include European political parties, public opinion of political processes, and theories of representative democracy.

At the University of Twente I was part of the Dutch European Social Survey and involved in the design and analyses of several population-based survey experiments that answer methodological and substantive research questions. I am also the co-principal investigator of the 2015 Swedish Party Membership survey. It contains information on political attitudes and behaviour of more than 10,000 members of seven political parties.

I am a volunteer at d|part - Think Tank for Political Participation.
